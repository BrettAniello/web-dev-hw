Why the Goblet Squat?

The goblet squat is a very basic, yet important, squat variation. The reason why it is such a great squat variation is because it almost forces your body to use the correct form while strengthening it. If everyone learned to squat correctly, and got stonger, the world would have many less bad backs.

Below is a sample 3 week program for the goblet squat.

Week Sets x Reps
1 3 x 8
Week 2 4 x 8
Week 3 4x10
