How It All Started

Bretts Treks started in 2016 when I broke my collar bone in a snowboarding accident. I needed a low impact hobby that would allow me to stay active, and it also allowed me to explore my creative side. I have never been the most creative person but I really enjoyed taking pictures and editing them to create beautiful images. Bretts Treks also pushed me to travel new places, from hiking the California Coast, to backpacking deep in the Sierra Nevada Mountain Range.
